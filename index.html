About DR TECH

Founded in 2010, DR TECH has grown from a small computer repair shop to a leading provider of comprehensive IT solutions. Our mission is to deliver exceptional technical support with honesty, integrity, and a commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our Values

  • Quality: We use only the highest quality parts and follow industry best practices for all repairs.

  • Transparency: No hidden fees or surprises - we provide clear pricing before any work begins.

  • Expertise: Our technicians are certified professionals with years of experience.

  • Customer Focus: Your satisfaction is our top priority - we stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties.

Our Team

DR TECH employs a team of 25 certified technicians with expertise across all major platforms and devices. We invest heavily in continuous training to stay ahead of the latest technological developments.
